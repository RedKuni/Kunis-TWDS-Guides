In-game Events

Here we list some of the events that require some attention to get better rewards and/or progress faster:

Tides of the Dead (Tides/TOTD)
  1. Find a partner with similar Formation 1 (F1) Combat Power.
  2. Move your town to the meetup location (defined by your leaders).
  3. Before the event starts, exchange F1s with your partner.
  4. Recall, replanish and send back your troops whenever necessary.

Before the event, look up which difficulty your event will be and find a partner that have the same difficulty level and approximately the same Combat Power (CB) as your's Formation 1 (F1).
Before it starts, you and your partner will send each other's F1 as reinforcement.

During the event, all the clan members will be attacked 20 times (waves) of Walkers, with each wave being stronger than the last one, with 2 minutes between the attacks.

Stay online during the event and right after a wave hits, ponder if your troops can handle the next attack. If you think that you need to restock your troops, recall them, replenish your troops and send it back as soon as possible. Because of that, it is recommended that you and your partner have your towns close to each other (your leaders will set up meeting points so everyone can get together and send their spare troops to whoever needs it).

The Whisperers
  1. Before starting, select your event difficulty.
  2. At level 5, 10, 15 and 20 the enemy is way stronger than the other levels.
    For rallies against those levels, always send your F1 to help.
  3. Against the Whisperers Boss, only attack with F1. Sharpshooters is preferred.
  4. Attack the Whisperers Boss 10 times, if possible.

Whisperers have two parts, a solo and a clan part:

Solo:
Here, you will have to defeat 20 enemies, which gets stronger each level.
Every 5 levels, there is a kind of mini-boss. So, at levels 5, 10, 15 and 20 is recommended to ask for help and/or rallying.
Remind that you can and should select your event difficulty before starting. After defeating all 20 levels of a given difficulty, it will pre-select a higher tier in the next event.
The higher the difficulty, the better are the rewards.

Clan Boss:
Here, your leaders will select a difficulty and schedule for the Whisperers Boss. On the determined date, the boss will be open for attacks. It will stay still for up to 12 hours or until he is defeated.
You have up to 10 attacks: 5 free attacks and another 5 for 100 rubies each attack.
You should go for 10 attacks whenever possible.
Sharpshooters gets more kills/points against this boss.

Tales of Survival (Tales)
  1. Follow the markers/tags ascending number order.
  2. Only attack ONCE mission with bread as reward.

There are two main goals: Defeat the map's boss fast and, after that, clear as many buildings as possible.

Your leaders will place markers/tags indicating which building the clan members should focus on.
In the first part of the event, the leaders might change constantly the markers placed on the map, as the main goal is to reach and kill the boss, it will trace the fastest path to it.
Please, follow their orders. It will make the event progress faster and give move points to the event.

It's important to note that some missions asks for a certain number of attacks to complete it. Those missions only gives the listed prizes only ONCE per participante, meaning that it doesn't matter if you attack it 1 or 10 times, you will get always the same number of rewards for its completion.

Clan Technology Donations

Donate to the Clan's Technology whenever you log in.

Clan Technologies benefits all the its members, you included. The donations is nothing special, just some veggies or lumbers. You can donate using rubies, but it's not necessary. Just donate to wherever is marked with a stared thumbs up. When the technology node reaches the required resource amount, an Rank 4 (R4) from you clan can start the research and mark another technology node.
